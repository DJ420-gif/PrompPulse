Where Perplexity AI Genuinely Wins

The citation model is what makes Perplexity uniquely trustworthy. Unlike Claude or ChatGPT which might hallucinate sources, every Perplexity answer links to the actual pages it used. Pro Search takes this further — decomposing complex research questions into sub-queries, searching each one, and synthesising a comprehensive answer with full source attribution.

02

Where It Falls Short

Perplexity is not built for code generation or creative tasks. Ask it to write a React component and you will be disappointed. It is a research tool, not a coding assistant. The free tier is also more limited than Claude or ChatGPT's free options for heavy users.

03

Final Verdict

Perplexity is the best tool available for research, fact-checking and staying current with rapidly evolving topics like AI itself. If you spend significant time researching before building, it belongs in your toolkit alongside Claude. Do not use it as a coding assistant — that is not what it is designed for.
