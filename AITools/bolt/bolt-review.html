01

Where Bolt.new Genuinely Wins

For proof-of-concept work and investor demos, Bolt.new is exceptional. The speed from idea to deployed URL is unmatched — a complete app with authentication, database and UI in under 10 minutes. Non-technical founders can build and show working products without a single line of hand-written code. The instant Vercel deployment removes all DevOps friction.

02

Where It Falls Short

Token limits hit fast on complex projects. Once you exceed the context limit, the AI loses coherence and starts generating inconsistent code. For anything beyond a simple CRUD application with a few screens, you will hit this wall. The output is also not always production-quality — it works but often needs significant refactoring before shipping to real users.

03

Final Verdict

Bolt.new is the right tool for rapid prototyping, investor demos and non-technical founders who need to show a working product. For production applications, treat its output as a starting point to hand off to an engineer — not a finished product.
